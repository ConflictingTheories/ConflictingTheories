About Spirit & Flame

Spirit & Flame was started with the vision of being the #1 online shop for all things aromatic. With a keen interest and high respect for traditional spiritual practices across the globe, we have come to have a deep love of aromatic scents and sacred fragrances. This world blesses us with so many joys, and none are both so subtle and so blunt as the sense of smell. It can bring us bliss and help us detach from the stresses of life with nothing more than a few inhales of a heavenly scent, whilst negative odours can disrupt our moods and burden us with stress.

By curating beautiful and sacred scents and substances from around the world, we hope to bring you a sense of calm and tranquility. Many of our products have deep traditional lores and deserve our respect. Where possible, we have tried to provide a brief description of these traditions, but in no way can we possibly cover the depth and breadth of such a subject and each religion would require their own site of information each just to do it justice. So whilst, we cannot offer an academic treatise, we hope we can inspire you to explore these heavenly fragrances, and get to know a bit more about their traditions and craft your own respect for them in the process.

Mission

Our mission is what drives us to do everything possible to expand human potential. With our efforts and these beautiful offerings we hope that we can bring some magick into your life and give you a moment of calm. We wish everyone peace, and hope that our scents inspire you in wonderful ways. Thank you for your time and looking at our wares, we appreciate you very much!
